METHOD - Component Parts, Materials and Tools

 

Required tools:

  • 3D printer
  • Wire Strippers
  • Screw Drivers
  • Pliers
  • Carpet Knive/Box Cutter/Exacto Knife
  • Soldering iron
  • Die tool(for making threads on potentiomer shafts)
  • Helping hands (optional)

Required Parts:

  • Linear Taper Potentiometers(one per articulated joint)
  • Bolts that match the die thread to be used on the potentiometer shafts
  • 20g wire(stranded)
  • Bolts/Nuts(two per articulated joint, plus additional for split parts)
  • Hot Glue / Cable Management brackets(optional)
  • Arduino board(Chose the type based on the number of joints to be articulated, you need enough analog ports or an additional digital multiplexer chip)
  • bread board

Required Material

  • 3D printing material - both PLA and ABS have been used successfully in printing a ROBOPuppet
  • Hot Glue(optional)
  • Solder
  • Foam sheeting(craft foam is acceptable)

NOTES:

Many of the parts used to make ROBOPuppet depend on the targeted robot. For example, a ROBOPuppet with smaller joints should use smaller potentiometers in those joints. A ROBOPuppet with joints that experience larger amounts of torque should use larger, more robust potentiometers.

 

 

Part Examples:
