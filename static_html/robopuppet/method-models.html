METHOD - 3D Parts

Below are links to download the pocket geometry 3D models to be used in modifying the models of the Puppet in order to fit and affix the Joint Encoder Assemblies, Shafts and Adjustment bolts.

  • Joint Encoder Pocket
  • Joint Encoder Pocket - with wiring tunnel
  • Joint Axis Pocket
  • Joint Axis Pocket - with friction bolt access tunnel
  • Bracket - rough bracket to be used with the JEP, users who are comfortable with 3D modeling may find it useful to model their own in order to better fit on the joint surface face. This bracket will only work for joints where the axis of rotation is centered around the joint encoder pocket.
