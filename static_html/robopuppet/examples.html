Stäubli - TX90L Robotic Arm

 

The first ROBOPuppet was designed and created to work with the Stäubli TX90L 6DOF Industrial Robotic Arm. This process was described in A. Eilering, G. Franchi,and K. Hauser. ROBOPuppet: Low-Cost, 3D Printed Miniatures for Teleoperating Full-Size Robots, To appear in IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems, September 2014.
